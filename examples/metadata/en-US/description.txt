LocalizerX is a powerful CLI tool for translating your iOS and macOS app metadata for the App Store.

Features:
- Translate Xcode String Catalogs (.xcstrings files)
- Translate fastlane metadata (name, subtitle, description, keywords, etc.)
- Powered by Google's Gemini AI for accurate translations
- Preserve placeholders and formatting
- Character limit validation for App Store fields
- Support for all App Store Connect languages

Getting started is easy:
1. Set your GEMINI_API_KEY environment variable
2. Run: localizerx metadata --to de-DE,fr-FR,ja

LocalizerX respects App Store character limits and warns you when translations exceed them. You can choose to auto-truncate, show warnings, or stop on limit errors.

Perfect for indie developers and teams who want to localize their apps without manual translation work.
